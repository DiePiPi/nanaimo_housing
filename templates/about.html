Our Purpose

Vancouver Island Solutions enables you and your team to deliver value quickly, with high quality, using the tools that your employees love (or free open source tool that we can customize for you).

Vancouver Island Solutions provides insights for data-driven enterprises, enabling them to support data analytics that can be quickly and robustly adapted to meet evolving requirements. Vancouver Island Solutions is an information technology (IT), data warehousing, data science and analytic team who knows that in order to meet the needs of its customers, data analytics has to be ten times more nimble and robust than it is today.

You know that the traditional slow and fragmented analytic process does not meet your needs. Your team needs an integrated database updated daily, the ability to make changes to that database weekly, and the confidence to use a customized dashboard to make changes real-time.
